petee-hand — character sheet

Write each character once in its box, in a black fine-tip pen (Sharpie pen, Micron, or a gel pen — not pencil, not light blue).

  1. Sit each letter on the solid line (the baseline). Lowercase bodies fill up to the dashed middle line; tails like g, y, p hang below.
  2. Write naturally — your hand, not careful printing. One clean version of each.
  3. The light-blue lines and the little gray label won't show up in the font; only your black ink does.
  4. When done, scan the pages (best) or photograph flat in even light, straight-on. Drop them in ~/homelab-data/petee-font/scans/ and tell me.
